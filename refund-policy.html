Refund Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

⚠️ Template notice: this policy is a starting draft. Consumer-protection law in some regions grants refund rights that override "all sales final" wording — have this reviewed by a qualified professional for your jurisdiction before you rely on it.

ChartCompass is a digital product delivered instantly. Because you receive the full indicator the moment your payment is confirmed, all sales are final and we generally do not offer refunds.

Why

Once access to the invite-only script is granted, the product cannot be "returned" — this protects every trader in our community and keeps the product fairly priced.

Exceptions we will look at

  • Access never delivered. If you paid but were never granted access and we cannot resolve it, we will refund you.
  • Duplicate/accidental charge. If you were charged more than once for the same order, we will refund the extra payment.

Crypto refunds are sent to the wallet you paid from and are subject to network fees and the value at the time of refund.

How to request

Email support@gouzhuang748.com with your order ID and payment proof. We aim to respond within a few business days.
