This Privacy Policy explains what information ChartCompass ("we", "us") collects when you visit our website (gouzhuang748.com) or purchase our TradingView indicator, and how we use it.

How we use it

  • To deliver the product (add your TradingView username to the invite-only script).
  • To provide support and send purchase-related communication.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share only what is necessary with service providers who help us operate (payment processing, hosting, messaging), and where required by law.

Retention

We keep order and delivery records for as long as needed to provide the product and meet legal or accounting requirements, then delete or anonymize them.
